Home

We are a group of Enthusiastic engineers looking forward to the appication of ML/AI in advanced disease prediction. Yes, it is gonna be a long way ahead but with your help and determination I am pretty sure that we can pull down the time this would take. My name is Adithya Naryan and i would like to introduce you guys to what me and my friend Mukka Deepak has thought of. Medical field is one of the areas that has critcal but limited use of AI/ML. I can givve you real examples , just check out the AI/ML disease predictors existing in the present situation. I bet that most of what you would find is research papers, and other stuff. I can guarantee that there is not even one single proper AI that can predict diseases that is being put to use currently. Why is this ? Well I believe that this is because most people are afraid of using AI to do something doctors could do. Many of you who are reading this may think we have doctors that we trust then why an AI to predict diseases. Well I am not suggesting a system where the AI does the job of the doctor. We here aim at making a system that can assist the doctor. Doctors are of many qualifications and many of them constantly are in need of a second suggestion (who wouldnt want one). A doctor prescribes tests depending on his or her perspective of what the disease could be . We try to provide suggestions so that the test conducrted are more in respins to the particular condition the person is suffering from. The next question that arises in your mind maybe how feasible is this idea. Well as i said this is a long term objective. I have done a little research and i can say that data related to diseases and the conditions of the humans that were affected is not so easy to find and process. Inorder to do this all, and make this ML/AI possible We have come up with this startup.
